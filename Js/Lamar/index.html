Kendrick Lamar's 'DAMN.' Album is Already Platinum

Kendrick Lamar’s ‘DAMN.’ Album Is Already Double Platinum

July 12, 2017 1:11 PM

It seems like Kendrick Lamar‘s winning streak sees no limit, as he has now earned himself another major accomplishment in his musical career. The To Pimp a Butterfly rapper released his DAMN. album back in April, and now, just three months later, it has reached double platinum status.

According to the Recording Industry Association of America, the critically acclaimed project from the Compton MC became certified double platinum on July 10, 2017 after moving over 2 million units in the last three months. One unit is equivalent to one album sale, a total of 10 track sales or 1,500 track streams.
